AGRIKIMA

Agrikima is a natural farming solutions company dedicated to transforming agriculture across Africa.


We empower farmers with sustainable animal health products and deliver impactful, science-driven support to build a healthier, more resilient farming future

Understand

We engage deeply with farmers and agricultural communities to understand their challenges — from disease outbreaks to productivity gaps. This helps us identify real needs and tailor sustainable solutions for diverse environments across Africa.

Innovate

Our research and development teams continuously explore natural, science-backed alternatives to conventional antibiotics. We craft effective, safe, and affordable animal health solutions that meet today's farming demands without compromising tomorrow's health.

Empower

Through training programs, strategic partnerships, and accessible packaging, we empower smallholder and large-scale farmers alike. We ensure our solutions reach even the most remote farming communities — because impact begins with access.

Sustain

We champion long-term agricultural growth by promoting responsible farming practices, digitized learning platforms, and farmer financing. Our goal is to build resilient food systems that protect people, animals, and the planet.

What We Do

Driving growth where it matters most — in the hands of the farmers who feed Africa.

At Agrikima, we believe real agricultural transformation starts with the farmer. That's why our focus is on delivering affordable, natural animal health solutions that are practical, effective, and built for the African context.

Natural Animal Health Solutions

Agrikima develops and distributes innovative, plant-based animal health products that serve as effective alternatives to antibiotics. These solutions help manage common livestock diseases while addressing the growing challenge of antimicrobial resistance, especially among smallholder farmers in Africa.

Farmer Training and Empowerment

Through one-on-one sessions and large-scale partnerships, Agrikima provides hands-on training to farmers on sustainable farming practices. By working closely with farmer groups and cooperatives, they ensure that farmers not only access products but also understand how to use them effectively for long-term success.

Localized Distribution and Access

Agrikima makes its products accessible by working with regional distributors and offering affordable packaging tailored to small-scale farmers. This approach ensures that even farmers in remote areas can access high-quality animal health solutions.

Sustainable Agriculture and Innovation

Agrikima invests in research and digital tools to promote sustainable farming. With a forward-looking strategy that includes digitized farmer education, market linkages, and strategic partnerships, the company is shaping the future of agriculture in Africa.

Organic Crop Management

Beyond animal health, Agrikima supports sustainable crop production through organic fertilizers and pesticides. These products help farmers improve soil health and crop yields while minimizing environmental harm.

ADVICE

ADVICE is a natural immunity-boosting supplement designed to protect poultry against viral infections like Newcastle disease and infectious bronchitis. Made from powerful plant extracts such as garlic, cinnamon, aloe vera, and echinacea, it strengthens the immune system, supports digestion, and promotes healthy growth. With added antiviral properties, ADVICE helps improve overall flock health. Administered through drinking water, it is easy to use and effective as part of a regular health management program.

AGRITONIC

Agritonin is a powerful liquid feed supplement formulated to support optimal poultry health and productivity. Each milliliter delivers a concentrated blend of key nutrients—including 5 g vitamin E, 30 g sorbitol, and significant levels of sodium, calcium, ammonium and potassium propionates, alongside essential amino acids like DL‑methionine, L‑lysine and threonine—designed to strengthen antioxidant protection, improve digestion, and enhance growth performance. Administered directly via drinking water, Agritonin is easy to use, making it ideal for routine supplementation and during stress periods to maintain vitality and robust health across your flock.
